About This App

HDSR AI Leadership Intensive — Course Hub is a personal study and planning tool created by Russell Riehl (2026) with assistance from Claude AI. It exists to organize preparation, weekly tracking, and deliverable work for the Harvard Data Science Review AI Leadership 3-Month Intensive (Aug 10 – Oct 21, 2026 cohort).

Attribution

Designed by Russell Riehl (2026). Interface and code developed with support from Claude AI (Anthropic), then customized and validated by the author. Course structure, dates, module themes, and deliverable concepts are drawn from Harvard Data Science Review and Harvard Data Science Initiative program materials.
